Lodging

There are a variety of options for accommodation in Annapolis. Fall is a busy time in the city, with Army-Navy games and other big events going on. We selected October 6 because it was an away game weekend, and a three-day weekend with the Columbus Day holiday on Monday, but unfortunately we didn't realize it was also the annual boat show weekend! So hotels will book up quickly. If you already know that you are definitely planning to attend our wedding, we recommend that you go ahead and make a reservation now.

We've arranged for block reservations between October 5-7, 2018, at two hotels, with a discount for the wedding party.

The special room rates will be available until September 5, 2018, or until each group block is sold out, whichever comes first.

Annapolis Doubletree Hotel (15 rooms)

$209 per night, with two-night minimum stay

For reservations:

  • Visit Doubletree's Briggs/Boone Wedding Room Block page; or
  • Call 410-224-3150 and use the group code "BBW"

SpringHill Suites Annapolis (25 rooms)

$209 per night with two-night minimum stay

For reservations:

  • Visit Marriott's Briggs - Boone Wedding page

  • Courtyard Marriott
