Ceremony

The ceremony will start at 5pm.

It will take place on a small beach, so wear or bring shoes suitable for sand.

The reception will be inside the center, which has an enclosed event room and an outdoor deck. The entire facility is wheelchair accessible. If you have any specific needs please let us know so we can make sure your visit is enjoyable.

After the ceremony, we’ll have a cocktail hour, and then dinner served at stations with informal seating. We’ll have live music and dancing for anyone who is so inclined.

Dress is whatever makes you feel comfortable and special!
