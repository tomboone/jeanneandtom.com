Registry

It's become a bit of a cliché, but your presence at our wedding is truly gift enough, especially for those traveling from far away. If you feel inclined to give an additional gift, we love experiences more than things—travel, good food and drink, theater and film.

Since we aren't taking a steam ship back to Burma, we can't bring a lot of physical items back with us after the wedding. Instead, we've crafted a selection of activities we'd like to enjoy together, and you can fund a single item or activity yourself, or contribute to group funds for one of the bigger ticket items.

Then, when we get the item or activity you contributed to, we'll take a selfie of ourselves enjoying it and send you the photo!

You can find all these suggestions and give us your gift on our registry site.
